Welcome to Gogle,

Motivated by a strong desire to create something helpful for people, our team at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham set out to make a search tool that can show short summaries of search results alongside the regular ones. Clicking on links in Google search can take up a lot of time, especially for searches that aren't really necessary. So, we came up with a new search tool and summarizer that can give a quick summary of a search, a YouTube video, and a webpage. This could be useful in many situations. And we believe that further research could make this process even faster. More information here. Meet our team.
